## üé® Design Philosophy Changes

### Before (Rigid & Traditional)
- Formal, corporate feel
- Limited interactivity
- Static content blocks
- Maroon and gold only
- Text-heavy sections

### After (Modern & Engaging)
- **Playful gradients**: Purple, pink, blue, yellow accents
- **Dynamic animations**: Hover effects, scale transforms, animated badges
- **Interactive elements**: Video players, photo galleries, stat counters
- **Varied layouts**: Grid cards, timelines, floating badges
- **Visual hierarchy**: Large headlines, colorful CTAs, emoji accents
- **Emotional connection**: Real photos, testimonials, stories
